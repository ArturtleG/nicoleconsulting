Topics

  • PLC foundations and practices
  • Mindset
    • Transforming how science is taught means more than just updating lesson plans, it requires a fundamental shift in mindset. Helping educators move from traditional, content-heavy instruction to more modern, inquiry-driven practices is essential to creating a classroom culture that reflects the evolving nature of science itself. With a focus on equipping teachers with a clear understanding of why change is necessary, grounding the shift in real-world relevance, scientific literacy, and the skills students need to navigate a rapidly advancing, data-rich world. Done effectively, educators can embrace innovation and curiosity, inspire the next generation of thinkers, problem-solvers, and lifelong learners.

About Nicole

I'm a passionate educator who believes all students deserve learning experiences that are meaningful. Over the past 20 years, I've worn many hats, yet in every role, I've stayed focused on what matters most: helping students think deeply, wonder freely, and feel seen in their learning.

I know how challenging and rewarding this work can be. That's why I partner with schools and educators to design practical, engaging, and inclusive solutions; whether that means reimagining curriculum, strengthening PLC practices, or leading transformative professional learning. My style is collaborative, responsive, and grounded in the realities of the classroom. Together, we can create learning experiences that are not only rigorous but also joyful.

I'm here to help you create teaching and learning where both students and teachers can thrive. Let's build something great…. Together!
